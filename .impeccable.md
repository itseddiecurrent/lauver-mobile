## Design Context

### Users
Athletes and fitness enthusiasts looking for training partners. They use the app while planning workouts or browsing between sessions — goal-driven, results-oriented people who take their sport seriously but want community and connection too.

### Brand Personality
**Energetic. Athletic. Authentic.**
The brand feels like a high-performance gear company — serious about sport but warm enough to feel like a community. Orange is the heartbeat: active, urgent, alive.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Primary palette**: Warm off-white background (`#F0EDE8`), muted card surfaces (`#EAE6DF`), deep charcoal text (`#1C1A18`), signature orange (`#E8602C`)
- **Dark mode**: Deep espresso backgrounds (`#161412`, `#201D1A`), warm elevated surfaces (`#2C2825`), same orange accent
- **Anti-references**: No neon-on-dark, no cyan/purple AI gradients, no glassmorphism
- **References**: Strava, Nike Run Club — functional, athletic, data-forward

### Design Principles
1. **Orange earns its place** — use it for primary actions, active states, and key data points only
2. **Warmth over neutrality** — backgrounds and surfaces are warm-tinted, never cold gray
3. **Data is the hero** — numbers are large and bold; labels are small and muted
4. **Dark mode is premium** — not just inverted; espresso-warm, intentional
5. **Consistency through tokens** — all colors come from the ThemeContext `colors` object (`c.BG`, `c.ORANGE`, etc.), never hardcoded
